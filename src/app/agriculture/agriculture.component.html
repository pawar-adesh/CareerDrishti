What is Agriculture?
Agriculture careers are professional paths related to farming, dairy, poultry, and animal husbandry. BSc Agriculture is a 3 year UG course providing all the scientific methods and techniques of agriculture. Students after completing BSc in Agriculture have a good scope in their careers. There are many kinds of BSc Agriculture Jobs available like Agricultural Officer, Agricultural Analyst, Plant Breeder, Seed Technologist.

These career paths involve everything from growing crops and nurturing the soil to raising livestock like cattle, pigs, and chickens. Several branches of agricultural science include crop science, horticulture, dairy, animal husbandry, fisheries, agronomy, and soil science. New reforms and innovations in this field have improved possibilities for increasing production and optimizing labor. The availability of professionally qualified people and technological advancements are primarily responsible for these changes.
